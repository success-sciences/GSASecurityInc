GSA Security, Inc is a low voltage electrical contractor (FL License #ES12000416) that provides physical security solutions including voice & data cabling, camera systems, card access systems, radar solutions, and assessment consultation services for very diverse clients in Florida.


Formed in 2005, GSA has performed extensive work for high profile customers in Hillsborough, Pinellas, Polk, Pasco, and Brevard counties. Some clients worth noting include Port Tampa Bay, Hillsborough County Public Schools, City of Clearwater, University of South Florida, Port Canaveral and other private businesses.


GSA has also invested in becoming a preferred Authorized Partner for solutions provided by AXIS Communications, exacqVision, Genetec, NICE, and milestone. Our professional technicians are trained and certified to enhance product effectiveness.

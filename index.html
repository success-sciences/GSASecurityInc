About Us


GSA Security, Inc is a low voltage electrical contractor (FL License #ES12000416) that provides physical security solutions including voice & data cabling, camera systems, card access systems, radar solutions, and assessment consultation services for very diverse clients in Florida.

We have 10+ years of experience outfitting local properties of all sizes with cutting edge security infrastructure—from access control systems, video surveillance systems, high end cameras, to repair and replacement services.

Formed in 2005, GSA has performed extensive work for high profile customers in Hillsborough, Pinellas, Polk, Pasco, and Brevard counties. Some client’s worth noting include Port Tampa Bay, Hillsborough County Public Schools, City of Clearwater, University of South Florida, Port Canaveral and other private businesses.

GSA has also invested in becoming a preferred Authorized Partner for solutions provided by AXIS Communications (staffed with 5 AXIS Certified Professionals), exacqVision, Genetec, NICE, and milestone. Our professional technicians are trained and certified to enhance product effectiveness.

We welcome the opportunity to meet with you and provide a no obligation assessment of your security options and vulnerabilities.

exacqVision

Exacq is a leading manufacturer of video management system (VMS) software and servers used for video surveillance. Learn more about Exacq Technologies' history and product line.

Exacq Technologies is part of the Security Products business unit of Tyco (NYSE: TYC), the world's largest pure-play fire protection and security company. Exacq is a leading manufacturer of video management system (VMS) software and servers used for video surveillance. Known for its ease of use, the exacqVision VMS software is available on factory-installed hybrid and IP camera servers, commercial off-the-shelf servers and directly installed on approved IP cameras. exacqVision offers a solution for every deployment from small, single-camera systems to large, scalable systems. Every exacqVision server includes the cross-platform server and client software, plus the exacqVision web service which enables the web browser client and the free Exacq Mobile app for iOS, Android and Windows Phone 8 devices.
